About Us

At our company, we believe in revolutionizing infrastructure maintenance with innovative and sustainable solutions. We’re driven by a passion for material science and the belief that advanced technology can transform everyday surfaces into long-lasting, high-performance assets.
Our flagship product, PMBSM (Polymer Modified Bitumen Stabilising Material), is a breakthrough blend combining polymers, bitumen, and surfactants. By harnessing the hydrophobic and binding properties of bitumen, the strength and cross-linking efficiency of polymers, and the compaction‐aiding characteristics of surfactants, PMBSM dramatically enhances the physical properties of construction materials. The result is a robust stabilising solution that lays a solid foundation for any project.
Complementing PMBSM is The Rejuvenator—a dynamic formula designed specifically to breathe new life into existing asphalt surfaces. This innovative product works to:

- Rejuvenate & Revitalize: It restores aged asphalt, extending the life of your road surfaces.
- Enhance Safety: Surface friction increases within 24 hours, providing safer driving conditions while sealing micro cracks for added durability.
- Ensure Seamless Integration: The formula is absorbed into oxidised surfaces without delamination, effectively becoming one with the existing material and retarding future oxidation.
- Optimize Operational Efficiency: With a fast drying time (allowing traffic access in just 2–4 hours) and the flexibility for repeatable treatments, The Rejuvenator simplifies routine maintenance.
- Deliver Sustainable Benefits: Its advanced design offers soil analysis-optimised adhesion, works effectively at a 15% dilution, reduces the number of monthly applications, cuts water demand, and lowers the carbon footprint—all while improving the working environment, visibility, and maintaining a productive, dry-running surface without harming brakes or undercarriages.

At our core, we’re dedicated to empowering communities by ensuring that infrastructure isn’t just built—it’s sustained through innovative technology and eco-conscious practices. Our mission is to provide solutions that transform road maintenance, safeguarding assets for future generations while keeping pace with today’s demands.
